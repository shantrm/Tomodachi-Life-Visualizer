What is this?

This is a web-based visualization tool for Tomodachi Life save data. It takes data extracted from your Tomodachi Life save file and displays all your Miis, their personalities, and relationships in an interactive way. You can browse individual Miis, view their personality traits, and explore the relationship network between all your Miis using an interactive chord diagram. This repository works together with the Tomodachi-Life-Data-Extractor to extract and visualize your save data.
